Status TAG Culture

Company work Culture is the attitude, demeanor, and shared vision that commits to the environment of an organization. Intriguing, enjoyable company culture can attract talent, and can also inspire employees to execute at their best. Additionally, a company culture clearly describes the workplace’s values and ultimately drives the entire company under a shared vision.


Work Culture at Status TAG is well defined by a few keywords, like friendly, motivating, challenging, nurturing, collaborative, engaging, and these are the factors that keep us stand out from the rest. Our company maintains a respectful and fair environment where one will find a proper work-life balance. We are a significant team of young people who see no boundary while performing their best and achieving the top. The zeal of youth in connecting with the whole world, in bringing a positive change in life, in creating a platform to express themselves, these are the few extraordinary yet straight forward characteristics which we look for our team member.


The company gives an unbiased opportunity to each of them to grow, and the company equally flourish when the employee grows.
